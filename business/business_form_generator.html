Browser-local business forms

Quick quotes, estimates, and invoices.

A light, private one-page tool for low-volume one-off documents. Companies, logos, draft data, and backups stay in this browser unless you export JSON.

Company

Saved locally. Use different companies for different senders.
Logo
Recommended: PNG/SVG/WebP, transparent background, square or wide mark. Minimum 300×300 px. Maximum practical file size 500 KB; the app will reject images over 1 MB because browser storage is limited.

Document

Print opens your browser’s Save as PDF flow.

Customer

Who this document is for.

Line items

Add manually or paste CSV.
Accepted columns: item number, item, description, price, quantity. Header row is optional. Commas inside fields should be quoted.

Totals & terms

Taxes and discount are optional.
Saved locally
Private by default · no backend · no account

Other useful document types to add later

  • Receipt / paid invoice — proof of payment after a job.
  • Work order — internal job sheet with scope, materials, and authorization.
  • Credit memo — simple adjustment or refund record.
  • Delivery / packing slip — ships with goods, usually without prices.
  • Purchase order — request to buy from a vendor.

Settings & local data

Everything is stored in this browser’s localStorage. Export JSON before clearing browser data or switching devices.

Good low-infrastructure features included

  • Multiple sender companies with locally stored logos.
  • Tax, discount, shipping/fees, and currency formatting.
  • CSV paste/import for item lists.
  • JSON export/import for backup or moving browsers.
  • Print-friendly invoice layout for browser Save as PDF.
